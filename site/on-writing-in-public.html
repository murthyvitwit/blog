On writing in public

July 21, 2026

Most useful thinking never leaves a notebook. Writing in public forces a higher bar: if an idea cannot survive being stated clearly, it probably was not ready.

This site is where I publish essays — long-form notes on technology, systems, and the craft of building things that last. The goal is not volume. It is clarity.

I write here the way I wish more of the internet still worked: one person, one page, an argument you can finish in a sitting. No feed. No scoreboard. Just the text.

If something here is useful, take it. If something is wrong, tell me. The point of publishing is not to be finished — it is to be correctable.
